About the founder

Ma Huateng, also known as Pony Ma, is a Chinese businessman who is the co-founder, chairman and CEO of Tencent.
Pony Ma is now ranked the "Richest Man in China" after Jack Ma.

A Brief History

Tencent was founded by Pony Ma, Zhang Zhidong, Xu Chenye, Chen Yidan and Zeng Liqing in November 1998 as TencentInc. Incorporated in the Cayman Islands.

Tencent is the world's largest video game company, one of the world's most valuable companies, one of the world's largest social media companies.

Games

The well-known games that Tencent Games has developed with their company, and other
companies they co-own.

PUBG Mobile

PUBG is an online multiplayer battle royale game on PC. LightSpeed & Quantum Studio, an internal division of Tencent Games, developed the Android and iOS versions of the game.

LoL

League of Legends (LoL) is a multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Riot Games for Microsoft Windows and macOS.

Fortnite

Tencent owns 40% of the video game company called Epic Games, which developed the popular game known as Fortnite and released it in 2017.
